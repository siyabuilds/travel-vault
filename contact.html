Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get personalized travel recommendations?

Simply use our search bar on the homepage to enter your preferences like "beaches," "temples," or specific destinations like "Tokyo, Japan." Our algorithm will provide tailored recommendations based on your interests.

Is the travel information up to date?

Yes! We regularly update our destination information, including local times, weather conditions, and travel advisories to ensure you have the most current information for your trip planning.

Can I save my favorite destinations?

Currently, our platform focuses on providing instant recommendations. We're working on a favorites feature that will be available in our upcoming user account system.

Do you provide booking services?

We focus on destination discovery and recommendations. For bookings, we provide links to trusted travel partners and official tourism websites where you can make reservations.

How quickly will I receive a response to my message?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our phone line for immediate assistance.

Can I suggest new destinations to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome destination suggestions from our users. Please use the contact form above and select "Feedback & Suggestions" as your subject to share your recommendations.
